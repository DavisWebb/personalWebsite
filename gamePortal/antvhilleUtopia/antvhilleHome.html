Welcome to Antvhille! You have won the presidential election, Congrats! You will be presented with issues throughout your term and will make decisions on how to solve them! These decisions' impact and reprecussions will be visible through several bars on screen including national approval, debt, and the resource meter of each district. District 1 is the 20% wealthiest ants, District 2 is 21 - 40%, so on and so forth. The national approval bar on the left represents the average of all 5 districts. Right now all of them are at 50%, but once you start, they will vary slightly over time and of course, change drastically based on choices the residents like/dislike. The button in the bottom right will skip to the next month and you should click it once you've enacted all the policy you need to for the month. Good luck!
